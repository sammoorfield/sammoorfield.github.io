About Me

I have been developing CGI and VFX since 2005. This has involved leading teams of artists through the creation of sequences for a range of formats, from 8K IMAX 15/70 film, DCI digital cinema, dome to television. In addition to 3D and 2D animation, I have also been responsible for establishing project pipelines, scheduling and documentation.

My role often involves on-set VFX consulting as well as offline post-production supervision. I have experience with the post-processing of live-action footage and the conforming of stereoscopic (3D) and 2D deliverables. My work often involves 3D tracking, VFX shot compositing, sky replacement, object removal and footage cleanup. I have also been closely involved with mastering deliverables for theatres, streaming services and television.

I have experience with the day-to-day management of render farms used across multiple feature length productions. I have experience with managing virtualised Windows render nodes as well as setting up and working with Windows, Linux (CentOS) and Mac desktop environments.
